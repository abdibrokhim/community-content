### Monster mash

Dive into the fantastic realm of Image to 3D AI tools and discover the best offerings available, including groundbreaking tools like [Monster Mash](https://monstermash.zone/). This open-source sketch-based modeling and animation tool enables users to swiftly sketch a character, inflate it into 3D, and animate it with ease, all within the sketching plane, eliminating the need for complex 3D manipulation.

Monster Mash stands out by empowering both experts and novices to craft rich, expressive, and deformable 3D models from scratch - all while remaining in a user-friendly 2D environment. This innovative tool simplifies the intricate process of 3D modeling by allowing users to create 2D regions through outlining and then automatically transforming them into soft, deformable 3D models.

_Is it free: **Yes.**_
