[Google Voice Recognition](https://cloud.google.com/speech-to-text) is a speech recognition technology developed by Google. It can be used from the Google Search app for Android and iOS, as well as from the Google Chrome browser. 

## 3. Apple Siri
   
[Siri](https://www.apple.com/siri/) is a virtual assistant that is part of Apple Inc.'s iOS, watchOS, tvOS, and macOS operating systems. The assistant uses voice queries, natural language processing, and machine learning to answer questions, make recommendations, and perform actions by delegating requests to a set of Internet services. 

## 4. Amazon Alexa

[Alexa](https://developer.amazon.com/en-US/docs/alexa/custom-skills/steps-to-build-a-custom-skill.html) is a virtual assistant that is part of Amazon.com's Echo family of products. It is capable of voice interaction, music playback, making to-do lists, setting alarms, streaming podcasts, playing audiobooks, and providing weather, traffic, and other real-time information. 

## 5. Microsoft Cortana

Cortana is a virtual assistant that is part of Microsoft's Windows 10 operating system. It uses the Bing search engine to perform tasks such as setting reminders, providing weather and traffic information, and finding local businesses. 

## 6. IBM Watson
