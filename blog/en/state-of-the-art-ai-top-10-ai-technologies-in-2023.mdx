## The list of AI technologies in 2023

### AI-Powered Chatbots

One of the most popular applications of AI is in the form of chatbots. These are computer programs that are designed to simulate human conversation.

Chatbots can handle customer queries, provide support and even make sales. Thanks to their 24/7 availability and rapid response times, chatbots are an invaluable asset for businesses.

**Technology you can use: ChatGPT, Cohere, OpenAI GPT3, AI21**


### Predictive Analytics

Predictive analytics is a form of AI that analyzes data to predict future events. This can be used in several ways, such as predicting consumer behavior, trends and financial markets.

Predictive analytics is a potent tool that can give businesses a competitive edge.

**Technology you can use: RapidMiner, KNIME**


### Voice Recognition

Voice recognition is a technology that allows computers to understand human speech. This is used in various applications, such as voice-activated assistants, call center automation and disability assistance.

**Technology you can use: OpenAI Whisper**


### Computer Vision

Computer vision is a branch of AI that deals with how computers interpret and understand digital images. This technology is used in many fields, such as security, automotive and healthcare.

**Technology you can use: CLIP, YOLOv7**


### Natural Language Processing

Natural language processing (NLP) is a form of AI that interprets and understands human language. NLP is used in various applications, such as voice recognition, text analysis and machine translation.

**Technology you can use: OpenAI GPT3, Codex, Cohere, AI21**

### Robotics

Robotics is a branch of AI that deals with robot design, construction and operation. Robotics is used in various fields, such as manufacturing, healthcare and agriculture.

**Technology you can use: PyRobot**


### Neural Networks

Neural networks are a type of artificial intelligence that is modeled after the human brain. Neural networks are used in various applications, such as pattern recognition, data classification and predictive analytics.

**Technology you can use: PyTorch, TensorFlow**


### Genetic Algorithms

Genetic algorithms are a type of AI that utilizes evolutionary principles to solve problems. Genetic algorithms are used in various fields, such as optimization, machine learning and data mining.

**Technology you can use: GeneticSharp**


### Fuzzy Logic

Fuzzy logic is a form of AI that deals with uncertain or imprecise data. Fuzzy logic is used in various applications, such as decision support systems, control systems and image processing.

**Technology you can use: FuzzyLite, FuzzyWuzzy**


### Expert Systems

Expert systems are a type of AI that utilizes knowledge-based systems to solve problems. They are used in various fields, such as medicine, finance and engineering.

**Technology you can use: CLIPS**
