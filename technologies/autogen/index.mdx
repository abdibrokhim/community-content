# AutoGen: Multi-Agent Conversation Framework

AutoGen is a groundbreaking technology that revolutionizes the way we approach AI-powered applications, specifically those utilizing Large Language Models (LLMs). Developed through collaborative research efforts from Microsoft, Penn State University, and the University of Washington, AutoGen offers a powerful framework for building multi-agent conversations that can converse with each other to tackle complex tasks.

## Why Use AutoGen?

AutoGen's versatility makes it a must-have tool for AI developers. Here are some compelling reasons to incorporate AutoGen into your projects:

* **Customizable Agents**: AutoGen allows you to tailor agents to your specific needs, giving you full control over their behavior.
* **Human Participation**: Seamlessly integrate human inputs into your applications, enabling a more interactive and dynamic user experience.
* **Complex Conversation Patterns**: Support a wide range of conversation patterns, making AutoGen suitable for diverse applications, from chatbots to virtual assistants.
* **Enhanced Inference**: AutoGen provides enhanced LLM inference APIs, improving performance and reducing operational costs.
